Milestone 1

User Stories

Users can logon to the site
Users can register for the site
Users can view other users pages
Users can view their own pages
Users can add stories
Users can delete stories
Users can view stories


Milestone 2

Users can add tags to their stories
Users can view story lists by tags
Users can search for stories by text

Milestone 3

Users can follow authors
Users can mark stories favorite
Users can view other users' favorite stories
Users can "recommend" a story with a short blurb
Stories will display all recommendations
The front page will display the top 10 recommended stories of the all time
The front page will display the top 10 recommended stories of the past 7 days
This will mark the release of SpiderSilk 0.1
